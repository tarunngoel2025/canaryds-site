Why Canary

There are firms that help you with technical consulting. there are firms that assist you with business consulting. At Canary, we believe technical and business consulting are two sides of the same coin.

This is why we came together to set up Canary – a team of like minded individuals with significant experience, powerful technical capabilities and a shared belief that the Why, What and How of any problem statement or business scenario should be looked at organically and holistically.

Whilst many teams may come from a similar point of view, we were also uniquely positioned to catapult Canary to fruition given our deep technology stack and technical competencies. At Canary, not only would you find some of the leading domain experts and industry leaders to gain insight from but you would also be supported by stalwart technology specialists who can help you achieve and execute the advisory at the ground level of operations.
